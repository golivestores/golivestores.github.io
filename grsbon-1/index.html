About Dajiang

Trusted Manufacturer Since 2001

Wenling Dajiang Storage and Logistics Equipment Manufacturing Co., Ltd. has been a leading force in industrial storage solutions for over two decades. Located in Wenling, Zhejiang — China's manufacturing heartland — our 25,000m² modern factory is equipped with advanced automated production lines, CNC welding systems, and electrostatic powder coating facilities.

We specialize in designing and manufacturing storage cages, steel pallets, stacking racks, and custom metal containers for clients across 60+ countries including Europe, America, Japan, South Korea, and Southeast Asia. Every product is built to international quality standards with full OEM & ODM capability.
